You Are Not Alone

A spacial puzzle game about cats and ghosts

Created in the tiny game engine puzzle script for a miniature game jam You Are Not Alone take the simple push puzzle genre and gives it a unique twist. Using a very simple pixel art style and approachable mechanics this puzzle game forces the player to think differently about their every move! Play it here on itch.io and see if you can collect all 10 flowers while helping your ghostly friends to find their resting place. Enjoy the cute story while remembering that you are not alone!
