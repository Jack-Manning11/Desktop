Challenge 5: Accessibility Concerns

The main accessibility challenge that I see facing my website would be the lack of attention paid to users who utilize screenreaders to interact with web pages. With the lack of overall structure, caused by a slew of assignments kind of thrown together, and the index.html page not providing enough detailed information on the goal of the page it would be extremely confusing to try and interact with this page in just an audio format. In addition my alt text does not provide a truly detailed description of the images present, and instead states their purpose, something that might be better explained in a caption format instead of alternative text. That along with each "Challenge" only having a title and not a description, plus the form not mentioning instructions or which lines are and aren't required areas are the most prominent accessibility issues facing my page.
