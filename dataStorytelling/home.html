Judging a Book by it's Title...

A data visualization story by Jack Manning

This bookshelf represents all of the fantasy books that were analyzed as a part of this story, the full length representing the almost 15,000 titles

These blue books on the left represent all of the titles that fall under the "blank of blank" trope

This green novel shows the amount of books that are "blank and blank"

This purple section is every title that follows the structure of "The Adjective Noun"

This large red section is every book that has a one word title

This yellow book shows all of the books that are "The" one word titles

And this final gray section are the books that do not fit in any of the above categories, representing 43% of the books in this dataset
