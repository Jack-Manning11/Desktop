Letter to my Freshman Self

This project was an image book based on framing portraits around the lyrics to a particular song. I chose the song Letter to my Freshman Self by Atlas because of it's very deep lyrics. Since highschool I had always felt a connection with this song and it's one that I've kept close to me as I've moved on to college. All of the photographs were taken by me and I wanted to convey the feelings of the time period I was in, one of COVID and lockdowns, so almost all of the photos are taken within my room.
